Artificial Intelligence files transformation

Our client, an engineering services company, received files from their clients in various formats and with heterogeneous data, making the quote creation process slow and requiring significant manual effort to consolidate the information. This process not only caused delays but also increased operational costs and the risk of errors in quotations.

We implemented an advanced solution combining Artificial Intelligence and Robotic Process Automation (RPA), which now enables our client to manage orders autonomously, requiring only minimal human validation and reducing the time and effort required for the quote creation process.
